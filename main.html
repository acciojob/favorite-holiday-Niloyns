Kolkata


Kolkata, the Cultural Capital of India, is a vibrant city known for its art, literature, and delicious food. Located on the banks of the Hooghly River, it offers a perfect blend of heritage, tradition, and modernity. From colonial architecture to lively festivals, Kolkata captures the true spirit of Bengal.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Victoria Memorial – A majestic monument that showcases the colonial history of India.
  2. Howrah Bridge – The iconic bridge connecting Kolkata and Howrah, a symbol of the city.
  3. Indian Museum – The oldest museum in India with fascinating historical artifacts.
  4. Kalighat Temple – A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Kali, attracting devotees from all over.
  5. Park Street – Known for its restaurants, nightlife, and festive spirit during Christmas.

Must-Try Foods

  • Rosogolla – A soft and spongy Bengali sweet that melts in your mouth.
  • Kathi Roll – A famous street food made with paratha and spicy fillings.
  • Macher Jhol – Traditional Bengali fish curry served with rice.
  • Phuchka – Crispy puris filled with spicy tamarind water and mashed potatoes.
  • Misti Doi – A sweetened yogurt delicacy unique to Bengal.
